South Indian Thali

Typical dishes include rice, dal, vegetables, roti, papad, dahi (yogurt), small amounts of chutney or pickle, and a sweet dish.

North Indian Thali

A north Indian thali is a full platter typically containing a dal, a vegetable curry, roti, jeera rice, papad, salad, dahi, and a sweet dish.

Maharashtra Thali

Maharashtra Thali will generally be simple chapati, dal, rice, dry vegetables, chutney, and koshimbi, Matha, Sol kadhi, Piyush are served as beverages.

Bengali Thali

Bengali Thali has an assortment of vegetarian items such as Ucche Bhaja, Aloo Poshto, Cholar Dal, Begun Bhaja, Chanar Dalna, sweet Rosogolla, Aam Pora Sherbat, and more.

Assam Thali

It essentially comprises of rice, roti or puri served with accompaniments in small katoris (bowls) and Other meats include squab, duck, chicken, goat meat, and turtle on a thali.

Kerala Thali

A traditional Keralan thali is called sadya. It is served on a banana leaf and features rice, sambar, curd, kosumri, kootu and payasam..
